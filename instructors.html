Meet Your Teachers

Our instructors are master potters with a passion for sharing the art of ceramics. With over 20 years of combined experience, they are here to guide you through every pinch, coil, and throw.

Sarah Chen

Lead Instructor & Studio Founder

MFA in Ceramics from UCLA. 15 years teaching experience. Sarah specializes in functional stoneware and traditional reduction firing techniques. She believes that pottery is a moving meditation that anyone can master with patience.

  • Specialties: Wheel Throwing, Glaze Formulation, Cone 10 Reduction
  • Classes: Wheel 101, Intermediate Throwing, Glaze Chemistry
Book Private Lesson View Portfolio

Marcus Thompson

Sculpture Specialist

A self-taught sculptor turned teacher with 10 years of professional experience. Marcus is known for his large-scale installations and alternative firing methods. His teaching style focuses on breaking traditional boundaries and finding organic forms.

  • Specialties: Coil Building, Sculptural Work, Raku Firing
  • Classes: Hand-Building Basics, Sculpture Workshop, Raku Firing
Book Private Lesson View Portfolio

Elena Rodriguez

Glazing Expert

With a background in chemistry, Elena creates our studio's custom glazes from scratch. She loves experimenting with local materials and teaching students how to achieve complex surface decorations.

  • Specialties: Surface Decoration, Soda Firing, Decorative Slips
  • Classes: Advanced Glazing, Surface Design, Soda Firing
