Studio Policies

To ensure a creative and safe environment for everyone, we ask all members and students to follow our studio guidelines.

Studio Hours

General Hours

Monday-Friday: 10am-10pm

Saturday-Sunday: 8am-8pm

Open Studio

Available for Members during general hours.

24/7 Access for Studio Artists.

Safety Guidelines

  • Wear closed-toe shoes at all times.
  • Tie back long hair when using wheels.
  • No food or drink near glazing or clay preparation areas.
  • Ask for assistance before using unfamiliar equipment.
  • Wash hands thoroughly after handling materials.

Kiln & Firing

Bisque Firings: Tuesdays & Fridays

Glaze Firings: Saturdays

Please label all pieces with your initials and current date. Unlabeled pieces may be set aside. Pieces left in the studio for more than 90 days will be donated or recycled.

Kiln Deadline: Items must be on the "To Be Fired" shelf by 6pm the day before firing.

Cleanup Expectations

We are a communal studio. Respect for the space and others means leaving your workspace cleaner than you found it.

  • ✓ Wipe down your wheel and splash pan with a wet sponge.
  • ✓ Clean all tools and return them to the designated racks.
  • ✓ Mop up any spills or clay dust around your stool immediately.
  • ✓ Ensure the wedging boards are scraped clean after use.

Cancellations & Refunds

Classes: 48-hour notice required for full refund or rescheduling.

Workshops: 7-day notice required for full refund.

Membership: 30-day notice required to pause or cancel membership.
