Common Questions

Do I need experience?

No! 60% of our students are complete beginners. We love teaching first-timers.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes that can get dirty. We provide aprons but splashes happen!

Can I take my pieces home?

Yes! Once they are fired and glazed, they are yours to keep. The process takes about 2-3 weeks.
